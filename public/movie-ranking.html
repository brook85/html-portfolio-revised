The Best Movies According To Bruke!!!

The Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

The Passion Of Christ

I like The Passion of the Christ because it shows in vivid detail the suffering of Jesus Christ, which is at the heart of my faith as an Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Christian. Watching His pain and sacrifice reminds me of what He endured for all of us, and it brings tears to my eyes and gratitude to my heart. The film helps me reflect deeply during Holy Week and makes me feel more connected to the traditions of my Church, our liturgy, and our ancestors who kept the faith alive. It is not just a movie but a prayerful, spiritual experience every time.

Virgin River

One of my favorite shows is Virgin River, because it beautifully portrays compassion, family bonds, and perseverance in the face of hardship. The way the characters care for each other, carry each other’s burdens, and build a sense of home reminds me of what we are called to do as Christians — to love and serve our neighbor. Even in difficult moments, the show gives hope and shows that healing and forgiveness are possible. It inspires me to value my own family and community more and to see the little acts of kindness that make life meaningful and full of grace.

Space 1999

Space: 1999 is special to me because it reminds me so much of my childhood — the sense of wonder, the mystery of the unknown, and the dreams I had as a child watching it. Even though the technology and special effects seem simple today, they carried me to other worlds and sparked my imagination. Every time I see it now, I feel like a little kid again, full of curiosity and awe about the universe and what lies beyond. It connects me to happy, innocent times and makes me remember how much joy and adventure stories brought into my life.
